Legacy of the Sodhi Family

Sodhi Family of Guru Har Sahai

The Sodhi family of Guru Har Sahai belongs to the direct lineage of Guru Ram Das Ji, the fourth Sikh Guru. The family has carried forward the legacy of devotion, service, and faith through generations. They are holding an annual mela every year, bringing together devotees from different cities across India to celebrate the spiritual heritage and teachings of the Sikh Gurus.
